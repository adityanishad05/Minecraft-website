About Our Club

Who we are and what we stand for

Our Vision

To create a community where students can learn, collaborate, and innovate through programming and technology.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a platform for skill development, project collaboration, and knowledge sharing in various domains of computer science and software development.

2018

Club Founded

Started with just 5 members meeting weekly to work on small coding projects.

2020

First Hackathon

Organized our first inter-college hackathon with over 100 participants.

2022

Expansion

Grew to 50+ active members with weekly workshops and mentorship programs.

2023

Current Status

One of the most active clubs on campus with multiple ongoing projects and collaborations.
